Divadlo lab (design)

Divadlo Lab is a theatre that presents the work of students from the Theatre Faculty of VŠMU to the general public.

They needed a website that could manage a large volume of content — including performances, news, and student profiles — while also reflecting the theatre’s experimental “lab” character.

As part of the Stella Digit team, my role was to design the visual system, defining typography, layouts, and overall style based on the theatre’s brand.

The key challenges were differentiating performances across multiple venues, combining strong visual expression with usability, and making practical information such as transport and parking easy to access.

“The Stella Digit team created a modern, visually striking and user-friendly website that can stand alongside many professional theatres.” — Dean of the Theatre Faculty
