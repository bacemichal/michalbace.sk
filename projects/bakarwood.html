Bakarwood (design & dev)

Bakarwood is a family business making unique epoxy furniture. Their challenge was to present their work in a way that truly reflected their philosophy – a focus on quality, local materials, and a personal approach.

After workshops with the client, we defined the core messages and designed a clear site structure. Together, we also came up with interactive elements, including a clickable sample tool that lets users experience choosing the epoxy finish for their furniture.

The result is an online presence that not only showcases the products but also communicates the brand’s values, makes it easy to get in touch, and is prepared for future growth (language versions, new products, or even an online store).

Service: ui/ux, webflow development, photography, content strategy.
