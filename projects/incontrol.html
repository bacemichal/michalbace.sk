Incontrol (design)

Incontrol integrates complex home technologies into a single ecosystem. They needed a web presence that validates their expertise for high-stakes projects, prioritizing trust over standard lead generation.

I focused the UX on answering two critical questions: "Who is responsible?" and "Who will service this in 10 years?" I addressed these primarily through targeted copywriting and strategic copy placement. By positioning the client as a single, accountable partner across key touchpoints, the design eliminates technical hesitation early in the sales process.

Due to agency constraints, the strategy relied on logical market assumptions. If I were to iterate today, I would include a clear collaboration process and a case study focused on long-term viability: specifically, how a 10-year-old system was upgraded to modern standards without any changes to the home's original electrical wiring.

The result is a sales tool that directly addressed technical fears and positioned the client as a secure, long-term partner.
