Notify (design)

Notify is a simple booking system for small businesses. The goal was to design a landing page that shows how the system works and highlights its benefits for both business owners and their customers.

Based on inputs from the Stella Digit agency (workshop, positioning, competitor and audience analysis), I created a clear structure that explains the booking flow in a few simple steps and communicates key advantages — icluding free SMS reminders with no hidden fees.

The final design clearly communicates the product’s purpose, connects both audiences, and shows how simplicity can deliver real business value.

Service: ui/ux design
