Feztmind (design & dev)

Feztmind represents the Slovakian parkour scene.

After the launch of a three-day parkour street jam — Spefezt, there was a need to share event details, introduce a new jam concept, and enable simple reservations – that’s how the first MVP was created.

Later, we added an English version for international guests and expanded the site with an online shop.

Today, the site is used to handle event registrations.
